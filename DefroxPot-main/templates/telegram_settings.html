Setup Guide

4 steps to connect your bot

1

Create your Bot

Open Telegram and message @BotFather. Send /newbot and follow the steps to get your API token.

2

Copy the API Token

BotFather will give you a token like 7123456789:AAF... — paste it in the field on the right.

3

Get your Chat ID

Message @userinfobot on Telegram. It will reply with your numeric Chat ID (e.g. -1234567890).

4

Test & Activate

Click Test Connection to verify. If a message appears in Telegram, click Save & Activate to go live.
